The Early Life of Ali Shaikh

Life in Bellflower, California

Ali Shaikh was born on November 28, 1997 in Bellflower, California to a Pakistani Muslim family. Bellflower, California is located somewhere near Los Angeles. His first few years were spent in a small apartment with his aunt, uncle, mom and maternal grandmother. In general, Los Angeles was like a luxury with Hollywood Sign, Beverly Hills, and mountains behind the city skyline was a beautiful scenary. In his childhood, he taught he should come back in his adulthood whether for internship or visit his old house.

Life in Walnut Creek and San Francisco, California

Life in Walnut Creek and Contra Costa County, California

After Ali moved out of Los Angeles, Ali's mom and himself along with aunt and uncle moved to somewhere between Walnut Creek and San Francisco. These cities in Northern California are beautiful with Golden Gate Bridge, Yosemite National Park, and other beautiful landmarks like San Francisco Cable Cars and San Francisco Bay Bridge. Life in Northern California he found as a child was same thing luxurious but healthier than smog polluted Los Angeles. He and his family would go out for a drive back and forth whether if it is Los Angeles or Sacramento. By 2002, his family condition went from good to bad to worse when his maternal grandmother fell ill and passed away. This condition prompted Ali's mom and him to leave for Houston,TX but they would come back later for his maternal uncle's wedding. Later, in his high school year, he would be back to visit not only Walnut Creek but San Francisco, Berkeley and Oakland to view more of landmarks.

Upbringing in Houston, Texas

Upbringing and Current Life in Texas

Ali and his mother moved to Houston,Texas to live with his father. Ali was sad first, but he easily adjusted to life in Houston. He would later do schooling in Liestman Ali's parents wanted him to study medicine, but Ali chose computer science instead as he was interested in computers.
